Experiment 4: Images, Video, & Sound Art

Imitate

I searched for two projects when I brainstormed this assignment. One was created by importing a video and displaying each frame of the image from left to right, another one posed a set of images as specific text by way of a two-dimensional array.

Integrate

I wanted to combine the two projects above to achieve the effect I wanted, i.e. to have each frame of a video gradually appear on the canvas and finally form a specific text.

Innovate

I reconstructed the two-dimensional array into the text I wanted, my name, "SHUCHEN LIU.", and I used one of my boss fight videos in my favorite game Hollow Knight to create my name.

Reflection

Completing this assignment gave me a better understanding of the image() of p5js.This experience was more than just coding, but I also greatly enriched my artistic creativity by touring tons of other p5js creations while brainstorming ideas.
